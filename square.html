🔒 Sideline Security & Compliance

Sideline transactions you can trust.

By integrating with Square, Fees Please delivers bank-grade transaction security without introducing administrative bottlenecks to your team.

  • No Business Entity Needed: You don't need a registered company or business number (like an ABN/LLC). Simply sign up as an individual, complete identity verification, and link your personal bank account.
  • Direct Bank Settlement: Fees Please never touches, holds, or routes your team's money. All player transactions are deposited straight into your bank account via Square within 1-2 business days.
  • PCI DSS Level 1 Certified: Player credit card details are processed directly on Square's secure, encrypted servers. Your players' sensitive payment data remains fully protected.
  • Automated Square Receipts: Once a payment completes, Square automatically emails the player a transaction receipt, providing a transparent financial log.

Square FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

How long does it take for money to reach my bank? +

Square settles payments into your linked bank account within 1 to 2 business days. If you set up a Square Debit Card, instant transfers are also supported via your Square dashboard.

Do my players need to create a Square account? +

Never. When players tap the checkout link or scan the QR code on the sideline, they are taken to a secure mobile payment screen processed by Square. They pay via Apple Pay, Google Pay, or standard credit/debit cards in under 10 seconds without downloading an app or registering.

Do I need a business number (ABN/LLC) to use this? +

No. You do not need a business registration or entity to connect Square. Social team organizers register as an Individual, complete identity verification, and link a personal bank account immediately.

Where do the transaction fees get deducted? +

The Square fee and the Fees Please platform clip are deducted automatically from the transaction amount before it settles to your bank. You do not receive separate bills for transaction fees.
