The Challenge

The Senior Admin Headache

Amateur senior teams run on volunteer hours. Chasing fees, tracking cash, and aligning bank details causes massive friction between players, team organisers, and club committees.

Treasurer Reconciliations

The volunteer treasurer spends hours sorting bank statements matching deposits labeled "Davo cricket fees" with actual player records.

organisers Fronting Cash

Match day umpires need cash. organisers front it out of their own wallet, then spend weeks chasing players to break even.

Bad Debt Drag-out

Fee collection drags out into January/February. Some players drop out without settling their season dues.

Proposal Part 2

Enter FEES PLEASE

FEES PLEASE automates the collection workflow. We connect directly to the club's bank account, streamline team records, and let players scan-to-pay via mobile browser.

No Player Downloads

Players receive a WhatsApp/SMS link. In 15 seconds, they mark availability and pay. No accounts or logins required.

Apple Pay & Google Pay

Integrates with Square. Card payments settle directly to the FDCC bank account within 1-2 days.

Availability Invites

organisers don't chase players. Send availability invites & selected lineup prepay links instantly.

0% Platform Clip

With the Club Pack, the club pays 0% Fees Please platform fees. Only standard Square transaction fees apply.
