Features

Everything your team actually needs.

No bloat. No enterprise software complexity. Just the features that make team management easier.

Real-Time Ledger

Throw away the spreadsheet.

Track every cash payment and card transaction in one live ledger. Know exactly who is paid up, who owes what, and your team's total financial position - any time, from your phone.

  • Cash and card tracked side-by-side
  • Player nicknames (nobody knows who "David" is)
  • Season-long running totals
  • Share read-only ledger links instantly
Player Portal

Players don't need to download anything.

Share a link. Players open it in their browser, tap "In" or "Out", and pay their fees - all in under 30 seconds. No app store, no account creation, no friction.

  • Works on any phone browser (iOS & Android)
  • One-tap availability updates
  • Inline card payments via Square (Apple Pay / Google Pay)
  • Custom team sponsor logo visible to every player (Plus)
Payments

No more chasing bank transfers.

Connect your Square account and generate an instant payment link for players. They pay by card from the portal - money settles directly into your account, not through us.

  • Apple Pay & Google Pay supported
  • Cash is always free to track (no fees ever)
  • 30¢ flat platform clip on Plus (instead of 2.5% platform percentage clip)
  • Player receipts sent automatically by Square
