📱 Offline Mode & PWA

Sideline proof. Zero signal required.

Fees Please is built as a Progressive Web App (PWA) that installs straight to your phone's home screen. Our offline sync engine lets you log cash, check rosters, and manage Game Day on the sideline even when cellular reception is completely dead. Everything syncs back automatically as soon as you're online.

  • Save the app directly to your phone's home screen
  • Log cash payments and lineup changes offline
  • Auto-caches upcoming match details when you open the app
  • Seamless background sync once a connection is detected
  • Zero data loss, guaranteed

💰 Team Wallet

Track your kitty and team expenses.

Go beyond match fee tracking. The Team Wallet (Kitty) tab lets you manage team funds, log shared expenses (like game balls or post-match team cartons), and track player contributions dynamically. Perfect for social teams who split season costs or run a weekly kitty.

  • Flat-Rate Court Costs — manage flat fees (e.g. $128/game) by building a team buffer
  • Reward Players — easily credit regular players with free games funded by the kitty
  • Team Expenses — pay for team jerseys, balls, or cartons straight out of the kitty balance
  • Track individual player contributions and surplus balances automatically
  • Full visibility into season cash flow and outstanding debts
