Game Day

Your match-day command centre.

Open the app on game day and everything is right there - who's confirmed in, who's maybe, who's out, and who still owes from last week. Preload rosters and schedules effortlessly.

  • Live availability tracking (In / Maybe / Out) at a glance
  • Debt & Credit Indicators - colour-coded red (owes) and green (in credit) badges
  • PlayHQ Import - paste your league URL to sync schedule and game times instantly
  • Smart Fixture Upload - drag-and-drop a photo, screenshot, or spreadsheet to populate matches instantly
  • One-tap cash recording & fee collection progress bars
Payments

Card payments, directly into your account.

Connect your existing Square account as an individual with no registered business required. Apple Pay and Google Pay are supported natively. Money settles directly to your bank - Fees Please never touches it.

  • Apple Pay & Google Pay - one-tap, no card details needed
  • Scan to Pay QR code - sideline card checkout via player's phone
  • Shareable checkout links sent via SMS, email, or group chat
  • Individual Onboarding - connect using a personal bank account and simple identity check
  • Payments reconcile instantly on your ledger
  • Cash and bank transfer tracking - always 100% free
Ledger

Ditch the spreadsheet. For good.

The real-time ledger updates automatically when cash is recorded or cards are checked out. See who's paid, who owes, and track your season finances in one clean overview.

  • Cash, card, and bank transfer tracked side-by-side
  • Flexible Fees - set different fee rates for regular members and casual fill-ins
  • Per-player running debt and credit balance across the season
  • Identify game-by-game positions (ledger vs. match court costs)
  • Log manual adjustments to quickly correct historic entries
Availability Hub

One link. No app. Done in 10 seconds.

Post your Availability Hub link in the group chat. Players tap it to update availability and pay fees in any phone browser - no app store installs, account registration, or typing required.

  • No player app download - pure web browser availability
  • Separate roles for billing protection (Account vs. Team Admin)
  • Privacy Controls - hide surnames by default (e.g. "Ash P.") on public team links
  • Player Dashboard - optional email login for players to inspect balance histories

Email Invites

Automated availability & match invites.

Keep players in the loop without chasing them in group chats. Automatically email player selection, availability status, and post-game payment summaries directly to their inbox.

  • Availability Requests - players tap a link in their email to log availability (In/Maybe/Out) instantly
  • Match Selection Invites - email selected lineups automatically, complete with advance pre-payment links
  • Match Finalisation Summaries - send post-game summaries immediately upon finalising a match, including ledger updates and outstanding fee checkout links
  • Native, responsive email layout optimized for mobile screens
Reports & AI

Email updates & AI Match Summaries.

Organisers and managers receive periodic email summary reports detailing upcoming availability lists and financial ledger status. Plus, let AI generate fun commentator reports (Dave, Rusty, Gaz, Shazza) to post straight to WhatsApp or Messenger.

  • Automated Team Summary Emails sent directly to team managers
  • Post-Match Email Reminders with integrated card checkout links
  • AI Match Summaries in various fun commentator personas
  • Resident AI Help Assistant - Dave helps you query ledger data dynamically
Team Wallet

Track your kitty and reward players.

Go beyond match fee tracking. The Team Wallet lets you manage team funds, log shared expenses, and track player contributions dynamically. Perfect for social teams who split season costs or run a weekly kitty.

  • Flat-Rate Court Costs - manage flat fees by building a team buffer
  • Reward Players - credit regular players with free games funded by the kitty
  • Team Expenses - pay for jersey orders or game equipment straight out of the kitty balance
  • Track player credits and surplus balances automatically
