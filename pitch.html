The Problem

The Organiser's Trench

Organizing an amateur social sports team is a weekly admin nightmare. The team organiser or manager spends hours in the "trenches" chasing availability and money from friends.

Chasing Cash is Awkward

Nobody wants to constantly nag their mates for $15 on WhatsApp or at the pub.

Bank Reconciliations Splay

organisers match direct transfers on bank statements manually against sticky notes.

organisers Front the Debt

organisers pay registration fees upfront and end up hundreds of dollars out-of-pocket by season-end.

The Solution

Fast. Simple. Automated.

FEES PLEASE removes the friction entirely. We provide a single interface that handles player availability and matches it dynamically to cards, cash, and bank collections.

Availability Portal

Players tap a web link from WhatsApp/SMS. No logins, no app downloads. 1-tap availability.

Instant Digital Payments

Square integration supports Apple Pay, Google Pay, and card entry directly on the portal.

Availability Invites

Send availability emails and "You've been selected" notifications with secure pre-pay links in one click.

AI Summaries & Sponsors

Custom AI match reports (Dave, Rusty, Gaz, Shazza) that players love, alongside custom team sponsorship logo placement.
