Showcase Your Own Local Sponsors

Social sports clubs run on the backing of local businesses—like the neighbourhood pub, sports store, or café. Yet, traditional sponsorship spots like boundary signs or custom jerseys are expensive and only get seen during match time.

Fees Please lets you feature your sponsors front-and-centre where your players look every single week:

  • Weekly Availability Banners: Feature local partners directly on the Availability Hub screens. Since 100% of your squad visits the hub weekly to confirm selection and check the team sheet, your sponsors get guaranteed, highly-relevant eyeballs.
