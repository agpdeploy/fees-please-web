In-App Feature

Showcase Your Own Local Sponsors

We know that social sports clubs run on the backing of local businesses—like the neighbourhood pub, sports store, or butcher. Yet, traditional sponsorship spots like boundary signs or jersey prints are hard to track and only get seen during match time.

Fees Please introduces **Digital Sponsorships** directly inside the app, letting you feature your sponsors where players look every week:

  • Sideline Checkout Exposure: Display sponsor logos on the payment screen when players scan the QR code to settle match fees.
  • Weekly Availability Banners: Put local partners front-and-centre on the Availability Hub screens which 100% of your squad views weekly.
  • Digital Receipt Footers: Link players directly to your sponsor's website or discount offer on every automated email receipt.
