FAQ

Affiliate Program FAQs

Answers to common questions about referring teams and tracking upgrades.

Is it free to join the affiliate program?

Yes, completely free. You do not need an active paid subscription or even manage a team yourself to register as an affiliate and start sharing your link.

What if I am a player or become one later?

Your affiliate status is permanent. If you are already a player (or subsequently join a team as an organiser/player), your affiliate profile and historical referrals remain fully intact and linked directly to your single account.

Can I refer my own team?

To prevent abuse, self-referrals are blocked. You cannot use your own affiliate link to sign up your own team. However, you will earn rewards for any other teams, clubs, or captains you refer.

How are upgrades and earnings tracked?

When a captain signs up through your link, they are marked as your referral. Once they upgrade a team to the Plus tier, the referral bonus is credited to your balance, visible in real-time on your affiliates dashboard.
